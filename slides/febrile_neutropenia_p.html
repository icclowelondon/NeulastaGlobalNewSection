Febrile neutropenia is defined as an absolute neutrophil count of <0.5 x 109/L or <1.0 x 109/L predicted to fall below 0.5 x 109/L within 48 hours, with fever or clinical signs of sepsis1

As early as the first cycle, cancer patients are at risk of hospitalisation and mortality due to FN2-5
