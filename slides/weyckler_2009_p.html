Adapted from Weyckler D et al. Clin Ther 2009; 31(5);1069-1081

Hospitalisation for neutropenic complications (narrow criterion) was 2.1% with daily filgrastim and 1.2% with Neulasta; hospitalisation for neutropenia, fever or infection (broad criterion) was 4.8% with daily filgrastim and 3.1% with Neulasta

  • Risks of hospitalisation for neutropenic complications of chemotherapy in clinical practice: 36% less likely with Neulasta® compared to daily filgrastim1
