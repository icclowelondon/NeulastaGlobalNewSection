Suboptimal chemotherapy delivery reduces overall survival in curative settings1

  • An analysis of 793 patients with breast cancer showed that more than 75% with an RDI ≥85% survived for 10 years after chemotherapy1
  • Being unable to deliver the planned dose intensity of adjuvant chemotherapy can compromise survival outcomes1,2
  • Overall survival is significantly improved in NHL patients treated with R-CHOP-21 therapy and achieving RDI ≥90%2
