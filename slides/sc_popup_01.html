Neulasta® is a well-established molecule

Neulasta®

Built around filgrastim, an established G-CSF[Molineux 2004]

Lipegfilgrastim

Originator compound built around a filgrastim intermediate, XM21[Lipeg EPAR]

Comprehensive body of evidence

More than 30 clinical trials comprising more than 9 tumour types in numerous chemotherapy regimens and FN risk groups[Gregory 2010]

Limited body of evidence

Only 2 pivotal clinical trials, 1 of which showed no significant risk reduction with lipegfilgrastim vs placebo for the primary endpoint of FN incidence[Lonquex SmPC]

N-terminus pegylation, to reduce interference with receptor binding, and to provide homogenous pegylation[Molineux 1999] [Molineux 2004] [Layton 2001] [Tamada 2006]

20 kDa linear PEG (polyethylene glycol), gave optimal activity in comparison with other pegylation options[Molineux 2004]

Attachment of a 20 kDa PEG molecule via a glycolinker to the O-glycosylation site at Thr 134 (that is, in the middle) of recombinant G-CSF
