With Neulasta® primary prophylaxis you achieve better protection from FN than with daily filgrastim1

A meta-analysis of five randomized, controlled trials (n=606) found that the incidence of FN was significantly lower for Neulasta® primary prophylaxis than with daily filgrastim*1
