Neulasta® primary prophylaxis helps you to achieve the planned dose intensity in dose-dense regimens1

  • EORTC guidelines recommend G-CSF support for dose-dense regimens in all patients, where dose-dense or dose-intense chemotherapy strategies have survival benefit.2 Despite this, 16% of NHL patients in an observational study (n=409) given R-CHOP-14 did not receive G-CSF primary prophylaxis*

Proportion of patients achieving RDI ≥90% when primary prophylaxis was given

68%

Proportion of patients achieving RDI ≥90% when no primary prophylaxis was given

47%

  • Reduction of the relative dose intensity is contrary to the intention of dose-dense R-CHOP-14, and can result in inferior chemotherapy outcomes1
