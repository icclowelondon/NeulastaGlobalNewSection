Neulasta® allows you to achieve ≥85% of relative dose intensity (RDI) of chemotherapy for over 85% of your breast cancer patients even after they have experienced a neutropenic event1

Being unable to deliver the relative dose intensity (RDI) of ≥85% in adjuvant chemotherapy reduces the likelihood of 10-year survival in breast cancer2

  • In a prospective study, 407 patients with early stage breast cancer receiving standard dose adjuvant chemotherapy were randomised at first neutropenia event* to management with dose delays or dose reductions versus G-CSF as secondary prophylaxis1
  • After initiation of G-CSF, patients continued to receive G-CSF throughout all cycles
